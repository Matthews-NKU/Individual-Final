Wintons Origins

Wintons origins are no secret, he was born on the moon at the Horizon Lunar Colony. On this colony they were testing genetic experiments on apes and one silly hamster to see if they would could be given a higher intelligence while retaining their animalistic qualities.

He was raised by Dr. Harold Winston where he gets his name from and his signature glasses. There he learned of science and the world, it inspired him and he wanted to learn more. He was always trying to learn about the world around him and he loved to gaze out of the window. He came to earth after the luncar colony project ended when the apes revolted against the scientists.

When he left the colony he began working as a scientist on earth trying to prevent more violence like what happened at the lunar colony. Winston then founded overwatch to fight against injustice and to end wars across the world. Working daily to improve the world through tactical operations and scientific research. Many others joined him on his crusade and they worked together to end the largest war mankind had ever seen, the omnic crisis. Winton saved the world and allowed for peace to prosper for generations to come.
